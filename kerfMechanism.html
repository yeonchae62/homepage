Kerf Mechanism

August 29, 2023

Description

The patterns created by the kerf can be viewed as a continuous and slender geometric shape that follows a specific angle as it twists and turns inward and outward. Due to this twisting motion, this pattern enables both localized (within smaller sections) and overall (across larger sections) flexibility in rigid materials. The example of kerf shape is shown as blow.
