About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Construction Science at Texas A&M University, pursuing dual graduate degrees in Construction Science and Computer Science. My research focuses on advancing construction automation through innovative human-robot interaction systems and telerobotic technologies.

With a strong foundation in architecture engineering from Seoul National University and interdisciplinary training spanning construction management, computer vision, and human-centered design, I develop cutting-edge solutions for safer and more efficient construction operations.
